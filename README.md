# Excel Practice Datasets

A structured collection of real-world Excel datasets for learners at every level. Practice formulas, filtering, pivot tables, dashboards, and data analysis with guided tasks and step-by-step solutions.

---

│       └── sales_data_solutions.md
├── intermediate/

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Choose your level** — beginner, intermediate, or advanced.
2. **Open the folder** for that level and read its `README.md` for context and tasks.
3. **Download the `.csv` file** and open it in Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.
4. **Work through the tasks** listed in the level's `README.md`.
5. **Check your work** by reviewing the `solutions/` folder once you have attempted each task.

---

## 🎯 Skill Levels

| Level | Folder | Key Skills Covered |
|---|---|---|
| 🟢 Beginner | `beginner/` | SUM, AVERAGE, COUNT, basic filtering, sorting, simple charts |
| 🟡 Intermediate | `intermediate/` | VLOOKUP, IF/IFS, pivot tables, conditional formatting, date functions |
| 🔴 Advanced | `advanced/` | INDEX/MATCH, nested formulas, what-if analysis, dynamic dashboards, Power Query basics |

---

## 📋 Prerequisites

- Microsoft Excel 2016 or later **or** Google Sheets (free)
- Basic computer literacy
- No prior Excel knowledge needed for the beginner level

---

## 💡 Tips for Learning

- Always **attempt** a task yourself before looking at the solution.
- Experiment with different approaches — there is often more than one correct formula.
- Use Excel's built-in **Help** (`F1`) or the formula autocomplete to explore functions.
- Practice regularly: even 15 minutes a day leads to rapid improvement.
